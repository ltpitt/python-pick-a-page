# Welcome to the Markdown Adventure!

You are about to embark on a **thrilling** journey to learn *markdown formatting*.

## What is Markdown?

Markdown is a ~~complicated~~ simple way to format text! It's used everywhere:

- GitHub README files
- Documentation sites
- Chat applications
- AI prompts

### Why Learn It?

1. **It's universal** - works everywhere
2. **It's simple** - just text with symbols  
3. **It's powerful** - creates beautiful documents
4. **It's the future** - AI loves markdown!

> "The best way to predict the future is to invent it." - Alan Kay

## The Three Riddles

### Riddle 1:
> I start as plain text, but with two stars I become strong. What am I?
> Answer: **Bold text!**

### Riddle 2:
> Use me with hashtag to make your headers great. What am I?
> Answer: *Markdown headers!*

### Riddle 3:
> ~~I'm not used much~~ I cross things out. What am I?
> Answer: Strikethrough!

**You answered correctly!** The golden key transforms into a *Markdown Mastery Badge*!

1. Headers (# ## ###)
